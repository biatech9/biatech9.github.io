ProLink Project Details

Description

  • Prolink is an innovative social network designed specifically for professionals. It bridges the gap between service providers and clients, facilitating seamless interactions and collaborations. Service providers can showcase their expertise, share their portfolio, and connect with potential clients. Clients can easily find and hire service providers based on their needs, fostering a productive professional ecosystem.

Features and Functionalities

  • Profile Creation : Service providers can create detailed profiles with skills, projects, and social media links.
  • Content Sharing : Share and interact with posts, projects, and updates.
  • Search and Discovery : Find service providers by name, location, or business type.
  • Interaction and Engagement : Direct messaging, calling, appointment scheduling, follow, and review to facilitate seamless and meaningful professional connections.
  • Hiring and Announcements : Announce job opportunities and hire workers through posts.
  • Authentication System : Secure user authentication for safe access.
  • User Management : Add, update, and delete posts, projects, and profiles.
