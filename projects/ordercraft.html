OrderCraft Project Details

Description

  • OrderCraft is a powerful application tailored for businesses with a focus on managing orders efficiently. From handling room and table assignments to tracking inventory and sales statistics, OrderCraft simplifies daily operations, ensuring seamless service and optimal customer satisfaction.

Features and Functionalities

  • Room and Table Management : Efficiently manage rooms and tables, ensuring smooth customer flow and seating arrangements.
  • Waitlist Management: Maintain a waitlist for each table, allowing for organized seating during peak hours.
  • Menu Organization: Categorize items into different categories for easy navigation and selection. Keep track of available items to ensure accurate ordering.
  • Order Management: Easily manage orders from placement to fulfillment, ensuring timely service and customer satisfaction.
  • Print Ticket and Receipt: Generate and print tickets and receipts for orders, providing customers with clear documentation of their purchases.
  • Employee Management: Basic employee management functionalities to track staff performance and assignments.
  • Sales and Stock Statistics: Access comprehensive statistics on orders, sold items, and stock levels to inform business decisions and optimize inventory management.
  • Client Credit Management: Keep track of client credits for seamless transactions and customer convenience.
  • Barcode Reader Integration: Utilize barcode readers for efficient item identification and streamlined order processing.
