FloraGroup Project Details

Description

  • FloraGroup is a comprehensive app that serves as a hub for medicinal information, offering users access to a vast repository of articles and resources. Users can effortlessly search and navigate through various groups of articles, gaining valuable insights into different medicinal topics. Meanwhile, administrators have the tools to curate and manage articles, ensuring that the content remains relevant and organized.

Features and Functionalities

  • Search Functionality: Users can search for specific medicinal topics or articles to find relevant information quickly.
  • Group Navigation: Articles are organized into groups based on medicinal categories, allowing users to navigate through related content seamlessly.
  • Article Reading: Users can read articles within the app, accessing detailed information about different medicinal topics.
  • Admin Article Management: Administrators have access to a dashboard where they can manage articles. They can add, edit, or remove articles as well as organize them into groups to ensure easy access and navigation for users.
